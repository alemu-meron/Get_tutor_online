What we provide

The relationship between student and teacher (tutor) plays a large role in the trajectory of a child's academic success and social development. Establishing a positive relationship with their teacher (tutor) helps a student feel more comfortable, safe and effective.

This website is for the purpose of student to tutor connection. It will be used to connect students with a tutor based on their preference. The idea to build this web site is raised from our observation on the problem. There are many who want to tutor and also want to be tutored but there was no connection. Even if the connection is created by brokers, there is gap when it comes to the payment and information. So this website pushes both parties to the win-win situation.
